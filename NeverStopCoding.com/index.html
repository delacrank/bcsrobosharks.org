About the Author

My name is Juan Carlos, in 2014 I graduated with BA in Computer Information Systems. Currently I reside in Brooklyn and enjoy programming on my offtime. My goal is to obtain an entry level position as a developer. This website is a sample of some of the things I have learned through books and other sources online. I created this website on weekends as a learning guide for myself and others. This site has many references from books I have read and below, I will cite many of the books and examples of code used from them. I hope you can enjoy reading and learning from this site as much as I enjoyed writing it. Hopefully if you are just starting out or if you are continuing your education, this will serve as motivation to move forward with your goals, reguardless of how daunting or time consuming they may be : )

You can check out my Github account here My Github.

How to navigate the site

If you haven't noticed, this is a pretty large website containing over 300 pages. There is a menu bar on the left column which contains links to the various sections, for those who are more experienced or need a quick reference point skip ahead to the section you need. Alternatively, you can simply go through each section clicking next on the bottom of the page.
